REGISTER WITH A PRO
A Performance Rights Organization (PRO) collects royalties when your music is played publicly. You MUST register to get paid.
✓ REGISTERED
ASCAP
Non-Profit · Founded 1914
Members: 1.1 Million+
Catalog: 20M+ works
Registration: FREE
Royalty type: Performance
Structure: Non-profit board
Issues blanket licenses to venues, radio, TV, streaming. Pays writers and publishers quarterly.
✓ REGISTERED
BMI
For-Profit (since 2022) · Founded 1939
Members: 1.4 Million+
Catalog: 22.4M+ works
Writer Reg: FREE
Royalty type: Performance
Pay schedule: Quarterly (2026)
Largest PRO by catalog. Went for-profit in 2022. Free for writers — publishers pay $150 fee.
✓ REGISTERED
SESAC
Invite-Only · For-Profit
Model: Invite-only
Known for: Precise analytics
Notable: Bob Dylan, Adele
Structure: Privately owned
Advantage: Higher accuracy
Smallest but most selective. Uses data-driven royalty calculations. Invitation required.
✓ REGISTERED
SGACEDOM
Dominican Republic · CISAC Member
Covers: DR + Ley 65-00
Rights: Performance + Mechanical
Required for: DR events
UNESCO: Merengue + Bachata
Network: LATINAUTOR reciprocal
Required to collect royalties in the Dominican Republic under Ley 65-00 (2000).
✓ REGISTERED
SAYCE
Ecuador · CISAC/LATINAUTOR Member
Founded: 1973
Members: 3,000+
Law: Código Ingenios 2016
Unique: Triple-stack system
Partners: SARIME + SOPROFON
Part of Ecuador's unique three-society system. ANY public performance requires SAYCE authorization.
✓ REGISTERED
The MLC
Mechanical Licensing Collective · USA
Created by: Music Modernization Act 2018
Covers: Streaming mechanicals
Registration: FREE
DSPs: Spotify, Apple, Amazon
Pays: Monthly
Collects mechanical royalties from all US streaming platforms. Required to get your full royalty stack.
📖 INDUSTRY LAW: ONE PRO RULE
You can only register with ONE PRO in the USA (ASCAP, BMI, or SESAC) at a time — but you need The MLC separately for mechanical royalties from streaming. For international markets, register with the local PRO in each country you release in.
